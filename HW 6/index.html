I LOVE PUERTO RICO

Aqui viva Gente

​Bad Bunny's "El Apagón – Aquí Vive Gente" is a compelling fusion of music video and documentary that sheds light on pressing socio-economic issues in Puerto Rico. Released in September 2022, this 18-minute piece begins with the track "El Apagón" and transitions into "Aquí Vive Gente" ("People Live Here"), a documentary segment reported by Puerto Rican journalist Bianca Graulau.

The Puerto Rican coquí (Eleutherodactylus coqui) is a small tree frog and one of the most iconic symbols of Puerto Rico. It is named after the distinctive "co-kee" sound it makes at night, which is a common and beloved sound throughout the island.
